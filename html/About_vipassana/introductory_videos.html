As a supplement to the explanations of Vipassana by Mr. Goenka above, below is a short video with subtitles in multiple languages that consists of a series of interviews with Vipassana students. They talk about how they first came to a Vipassana course, and what the practice of Vipassana has meant to them in their lives. The title of this film is “Practicing Vipassana: Meditator Experiences”. The interviews were conducted of students from various countries and were recorded at the Italian Vipassana Centre.
