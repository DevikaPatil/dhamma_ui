Ask-an-AT Program

A new Ask-an-AT program is currently under development using virtual interview rooms. Old students will be able to schedule a 15-minute interview slot with an AT and ask any questions related to their practice or the technique.

The sign-up for interviews will be very similar to the noon interview sign-up sheet on 10-day or 3-day courses, where old students can request a private meeting with an AT. The only difference here is that these meetings will be via a telephone call, in a virtual interview room. Once a student signs-up for an available interview slot they will receive an e-mail with a call-in phone number and an access code.
