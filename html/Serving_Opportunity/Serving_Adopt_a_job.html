Adopt-A-Job

If you don’t feel able to join a committee, but have a little time to do some Dhamma service, the following is a list of various tasks. Some are occasional tasks (like sewing) or seasonal (like putting up tents) and others have to be done each course (like day 0 jobs). Maintenance Committee

  • Change air filters and lubricate ventilation system in meditation hall and pagoda (every three-four months)
  • Service lawnmowers and snowblower
  • Organize tools
  • LOTS MORE!!! (Call for information)

Registration Committee

  • Help on Day 0 by showing students to their rooms
  • Make info packets (once every four months)

Kitchen Committee

  • Day 0 help-make new servers feel welcome and supported
  • Day 1 and Day 2 support for kitchen servers
  • Food ordering
  • Receiving and unpacking orders
  • Cleanup on Day 11
  • Cook in-between courses
  • Prepare kitchen for the next course
  • Help organize the kitchen for work periods
  • On call for support when needed

Household Committee

  • Every day 0 do a walk-thru to make sure all areas are clean and welcoming for the students
  • Every day 11 coordinate cleanup and do a walk-thru to see what areas still need to be cleaned
  • Inspect and clean tents after every course during the warmer parts of the year
  • Sewing and mending cushion covers,curtains etc.
  • Cushion wrangling-washing cushion covers, keeping them in proper order

Grounds and Garden Committee

  • Lawn mowing in-between every course in the summer months
  • Snow blowing in the winter
  • Preparing flower beds in the spring
  • Maintaining flower beds during the growing season
  • Moving trees and shrubs
  • Putting gardens to bed for the winter
  • Taking care of indoor plants (weekly)

Technology /Website Committee

  • Computer support for the office.

Tent Committee

  • Inspect tent platforms and fix as needed
  • Move platforms if needed
  • Inspect all tents and tarps and take broken ones to be mended
  • Set up tents in the spring.
  • Take down tents in the fall and store properly

Outreach Committee

  • Set up public events like video showings and talks to bring the Dhamma to a wider audience
  • Work on organizing an open house at the center and coordinate publicity

Communications Committee

  • Work on the website, adding information as needed
  • If proficient in a foreign language, translate and maintain web-pages in that language
  • Help on developing brochures for various center activities
  • Be on call for graphics work
  • Be on call for editing and preparing documents
