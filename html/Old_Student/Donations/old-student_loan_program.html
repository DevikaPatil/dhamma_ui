Old Student Loan Program

It is so inspirational to see the role of MAVA region in facilitating the spread of Dhamma. Last year 256 students sat / served courses offered in the region. In 2015 the number is expected to exceed 850 students. MAVA is now offering 20 10-Day courses and 8 3-Day courses.

Our ability to offer courses throughout the year has been possible because of the Regional Meditation Center at Delaware. These courses are always over subscribed and some times the waitlist is three to four times the current center capacity. The Center can currently accommodate 15 students of a single gender. Our goal is to offer dual gender courses at the Center with a student capacity of 40+ students by the end of 2016.

In order to facilitate this expansion the finance committee is complimenting the current dana stream with a very attractive Long Term Old Student Loan Program (OSLP) that will be beneficial to the old students as well as the Center. Personal Guarantee

A Personal Guarantee is a guarantee in which an Old Student agrees to be responsible for the financial obligations of MAVA to a lender related to loan, in the event that MAVA fails to pay an amount owed under the loan agreement. This commitment is guarantee for a future event and as such no money is given to MAVA at the time of signing this form. Old Student potential future liability is limited to the personal guarantee amount that is stated on this form.
